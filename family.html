Family

My family is living an average life. There are four members in my family: my mom, dad, younger brother, and me. I'm pretty close with my family.

First is about my mom. She's a strong, independent, strict, and fierce woman. She's the kind of person who's always on time and doesn't like to wait or make people wait, and she likes to plan everything first.

Next is about my dad. He's a chill and independent guy. He's quite fierce and strict too. He's very organized with everything and laid back. He doesn't like to rush things and takes things slow.

Lastly is about my younger brother. He's the youngest in my family. The age gap between me and him is 8 years. He likes to cause trouble and always teases me to make me angry. He's a funny, kind, and mischievous boy.
